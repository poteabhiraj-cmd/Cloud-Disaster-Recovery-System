Disaster Recovery Failover Plan

Primary Region:
- Asia Pacific (Mumbai) - ap-south-1

Backup Region:
- Asia Pacific (Singapore) - ap-southeast-1

Failover Steps:
1. Detect failure in Mumbai region.
2. Switch application to Singapore backup bucket.
3. Continue serving files from Singapore.
4. Restore Mumbai region when available.
5. Synchronize data back to the primary region.

Status:
Automatic disaster recovery process.
